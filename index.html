Don Toliver

With the raging success that was his 2021 full-length album, Don Toliver's Life of a DON gave Toliver a chance to truly express what felt like is his true self in the best way he knew how. For it's predesesor, Toliver takes a more experimental approach as he delves into the idea of love and all the feelings, experiences and deep thoughts that come along with it. With no shortage of heavy hitting records such as "Private Landing" (featuring Justin Bieber & Future) and "Embarrassed" (Featuring fellow Cactus Jack artist Travis Scott), smooth and slow classics-in-the-making like the Charlie Wilson featured "If I Had" and many more, Toliver Successfully takes us through all instances of the occurence of love in a way like no other!
